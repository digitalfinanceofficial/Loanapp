Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 Aug 2025

SmartCash Finance (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, whom we share it with and the choices you have. We comply with applicable Indian laws and RBI’s Digital Lending guidelines.

Data we collect How we use Lawful basis Sharing Cookies Security Retention Your rights Grievance

1) Data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to evaluate your application, onboard you, and service your loan:

  • Basic details: name, date of birth, gender, address, email, mobile.
  • KYC: Aadhaar, PAN, and other permitted documents for identity/address verification.
  • Financial info: income data, employment/business details, bank statements, and credit information from bureaus (e.g., CIBIL) as authorized by you.
  • Device/usage: IP, device identifiers, browser, pages visited, timestamps (for fraud prevention and analytics).
  • Communications: messages/emails to support, call logs with our agents.

Sensitive personal data is collected/processed only with your explicit consent and for legitimate purposes related to lending.

2) How we use your data

  • Assess eligibility, underwrite and process loan applications.
  • Conduct KYC/eKYC, identity and fraud checks.
  • Service your loan: communication, statements, reminders and support.
  • Comply with legal/regulatory requirements and audit obligations.
  • Improve our products, user experience and security controls.
  • Send transactional alerts; marketing is done only with opt-in consent and can be opted out anytime.

3) Lawful basis & consent

Processing is based on one or more of the following: (a) your consent; (b) performance of a contract (loan/ services); (c) compliance with law; (d) our legitimate interests such as fraud prevention and platform security.

4) Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Lending partners (NBFCs/Banks) for credit decisioning, disbursal and servicing.
  • Service providers (KYC, credit bureaus, analytics, cloud hosting, payment gateways) bound by confidentiality & data-protection obligations.
  • Regulators/Law enforcement when required by applicable law.
  • Business transfers: in case of merger/acquisition, subject to equivalent protection.

5) Cookies & tracking

We use essential cookies for session management and security; and analytics cookies to improve performance. You can control non-essential cookies from your browser settings. Disabling may impact some features.

6) Data security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest where applicable.
  • Role-based access controls, monitoring and audits.
  • Vendor due-diligence and contractual safeguards.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We promptly address incidents as per internal policies and applicable law.

7) Data retention

We retain data for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy and to comply with legal, regulatory, taxation, accounting or reporting requirements. After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

8) Your choices & rights

  • Access, update or correct your information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing/marketing.
  • Request deletion, subject to legal/regulatory retention obligations.
  • Raise complaints with our Grievance Officer.

To exercise rights, write to support@smartcashfinance.in.

9) Minors

Our services are intended for individuals 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10) Grievance Officer

If you have questions or concerns about privacy, contact our Grievance Officer:

Email: grievance@smartcashfinance.in
Address: SmartCash Finance, Delhi, India
Working hours: Mon–Sat, 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

11) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

This page is for information only and does not constitute legal advice.
