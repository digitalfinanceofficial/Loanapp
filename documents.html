Documents Required

These documents are needed for verification & quick loan approval.

Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory for KYC)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Voter ID / Passport / Driving License (if asked)
  • Utility Bill / Rent Agreement / Bank Passbook (for address)

KYC verification is done digitally via Aadhaar XML/eKYC & PAN validation.

Income & Employment Proof

  • Salaried: Last 3 months’ salary slips
  • Salaried: Last 3–6 months’ bank statement
  • Self-employed: Last 6–12 months’ bank statement
  • Self-employed: GST / Shop Act / Business proof
  • ITR or Form-16 (if requested by lender)

Eligibility depends on stable income flow & clean repayment history.

Additional Requirements

  • Passport-size photograph (for record)
  • Selfie (for online eKYC match)
  • Active mobile number & email (for OTP & communication)
  • Cancelled cheque or bank passbook copy (for loan disbursal)
