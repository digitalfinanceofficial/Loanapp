Refund Policy

Last updated: 25 Aug 2025

1) Processing Fees

SmartCash Finance, as a loan service platform, charges processing/service fees as disclosed upfront before your loan application submission. These fees are non-refundable once the loan application has been processed.

2) Loan Disbursal

Once a loan is approved and disbursed by our partner NBFC/Bank, refunds are not applicable on the loan amount or interest. Borrowers are required to repay as per the loan agreement signed with the lending partner.

3) Duplicate Payment

In the event of a duplicate payment towards processing fee or charges due to technical error, the excess amount will be refunded to the original payment source within 7–10 working days after verification.

4) Cancellations

You may cancel your loan application before submission at no cost. Once submitted, cancellation may not be possible and the applicable processing fees remain non-refundable.

5) Contact for Refunds

If you believe you are entitled to a refund under this Policy, please reach us at:

  • Email: support@smartcashfinance.in
  • Grievance: grievance@smartcashfinance.in
