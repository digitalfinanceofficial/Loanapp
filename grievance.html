Grievance Redressal

We follow RBI’s customer grievance guidelines. Raise concerns using the matrix below. We strive to resolve within the stated TATs.

Level 1 — Support
Email: support@smartcashfinance.in
TAT: 3–5 working days
Level 2 — Grievance Officer
Email: grievance@smartcashfinance.in
TAT: 7–10 working days
Level 3 — Lender/NBFC Nodal
Escalate if unresolved; details provided in your KFS.

If still unsatisfied after 30 days, you may approach RBI’s Integrated Ombudsman (as applicable to the lender).
